Tic Tac Toe

Instructions

  • Click on any empty square to make your move.
  • The computer will automatically make its move after yours.
  • Try to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) before the computer does.
  • If all squares are filled without a winner, the game will end in a draw.
  • Undo is not allowed!
  • Press the Game Icon to get back to the Home page.
