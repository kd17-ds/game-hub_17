Memory Game

Instructions

  • Click on the "Start" button to begin the memory game.
  • Flip over two cards to reveal their symbols.
  • If the symbols on the two cards match, they will remain face-up. If they do not match, they will be flipped back down.
  • Use your memory to recall the positions of previously revealed cards to match pairs more effectively.
  • The game is won when all pairs are successfully matched.
  • Your time and number of moves will be tracked as you play.
  • Click "Restart" to shuffle the cards and play again.
  • Press the Game Icon to get back to the Home page.
