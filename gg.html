Guessing Game

Instructions

  • Begin by entering the maximum range within which you'd like to pick a number.
  • Take your best guess at the number, keeping it within the specified range.
  • Your number of attempts will be tracked as you try to find the correct number.
  • You'll Receive helpful hints after each incorrect guess to guide you closer to the answer.
  • Once you've guessed the correct number, feel free to play again!
