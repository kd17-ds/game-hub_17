Simon Says Game

Instructions

  • Press the "Start" button to begin the game.
  • Watch carefully as Simon lights up a sequence of colors.
  • After Simon's sequence, repeat the same sequence by clicking the colors in the same order.
  • If you correctly repeat the sequence, Simon will add another color to the sequence. If you make a mistake, the game ends.
  • The game continues until you make a mistake.
  • Try to reach the highest score and challenge your friends!
  • Each level adds one additional color to the sequence, increasing the difficulty.
  • Press the Game Icon to get back to the Home page.
