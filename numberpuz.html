Number Puzzle

Instructions

  • Arrange the numbers 1 to 15 within the grid by sliding them into the empty space.
  • To move a number, click on it. It will slide into the empty space, allowing you to reposition the tiles.
  • Keep track of your moves as you strategize to complete the puzzle efficiently.
  • Challenge yourself to solve the puzzle using as few moves as possible and enjoy the satisfaction of a successfully completed puzzle!
  • Feel free to restart the game at any time to improve your strategy and beat your best score!
